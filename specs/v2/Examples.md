Combined Stat Decision Engine (Conceptual)
Decision Flow for "Will she engage in X encounter?"

Apply
1. CHECK LOYALTY BOUNDARY
   ├─ If Loyalty ≥ 100: Only spouse-inclusive scenarios (threesomes with consent)
   ├─ If Loyalty 80-99: Cheating only with trusted individuals, needs strong Desire
   ├─ If Loyalty 60-79: Cheating possible with trusted or moderately familiar
   ├─ If Loyalty 40-59: Cheating likely if Desire high, opportunistic
   └─ If Loyalty <40: Cheating freely considered

2. CHECK DESIRE THRESHOLD
   ├─ If Desire < 50: Only emotional intimacy or minimal physical contact
   ├─ If Desire 50-69: Vanilla intimacy, adventurous only with spouse
   ├─ If Desire 70-84: More adventurous, willing to explore
   └─ If Desire ≥ 85: Most scenarios considered, strong drive

3. CHECK CONNECTION REQUIREMENT
   ├─ How well must she know the third party?
   ├─ High Connection preference → only trusted partners
   ├─ Low Connection requirement → strangers acceptable
   └─ Influenced by Tension (higher Tension = more comfortable with strangers)

4. CHECK AGENCY (Dominance vs Restraint)
   ├─ High Dominance + Low Restraint → She initiates
   ├─ High Dominance + High Restraint → She wants to but restrains herself
   ├─ Low Dominance + Low Restraint → She yields when approached
   └─ Low Dominance + High Restraint → She resists seduction

5. FINAL DECISION MATRIX
   Combine all factors to determine:
   ├─ Willingness: Yes/No/Maybe
   ├─ Agency: Active seeking / Passive acceptance / Resistance
   └─ Conditions: What would make her say yes?
